# Privacy Policy for Sooral Cricket Board

**Last Updated: January 23, 2026**

## Introduction

Sooral Cricket Board ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Sooral Cricket Board mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### Information We Do NOT Collect

Our App is designed with privacy in mind. We do **NOT** collect:

- Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- Location data
- Device identifiers or unique device IDs
- Financial information
- Health information
- Contacts or address book data
- Photos, videos, or media files
- Microphone or camera data
- Login credentials

### Information That May Be Collected Automatically

The App may collect limited anonymous information for functionality purposes:

- **App Usage Data**: Anonymous statistics about app performance and crash reports (handled by Expo/React Native framework)
- **Device Information**: Basic device type and operating system version for compatibility purposes

This information is collected anonymously and cannot be used to identify individual users.

## How We Use Information

Any information collected is used solely to:

- Maintain and improve app functionality
- Fix bugs and technical issues
- Ensure compatibility with different devices

## Data Storage

### Local Data

The App stores cricket statistics and match data locally on your device for offline access. This data:

- Is publicly available sports statistics
- Does not contain personal information
- Can be cleared by uninstalling the App

### No Cloud Storage of Personal Data

We do not store any personal user data on external servers or cloud services.

## Third-Party Services

Our App may use the following third-party services:

### Expo

Our App is built using Expo, which may collect anonymous crash reports and performance metrics. Please refer to [Expo's Privacy Policy](https://expo.dev/privacy) for more information.

### Google Play Services

If you download the App from Google Play Store, Google may collect certain information as described in [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## Children's Privacy

Our App does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. The App displays publicly available sports statistics that are suitable for all ages.

## Security

We value your trust and strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect any information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
